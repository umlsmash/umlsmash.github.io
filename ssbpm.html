Super Smash Bros. Project M

Info

What: Our weekly Super Smash Bros. Project M tournaments, featuring Harmak, who's pretty good at this video game.
When: Every Friday
~7:00 PM
Where: USuites - Get Directions
Tournament Officials
Head:
Anna Mayorskiy
(Harmak)
Parking: There are metered spots on the side street to the right of USuites (as you are looking at it from the front). These meters aren't generally checked after 6:00 PM. If you get there any earlier, you should be fine if you pay $1 to the meter.
Food: You can get eat at Hawk's Nest (right down the hall from the venue room!). They have chicken fingers and the like, as well as chicken friend rice and lo mein. If you're planning on eating here, plan on spending ~$10 or less. If you're in the mood, they also have smoothies and frozen yogurt!
