Super Smash Bros. Project M

Info

What: Our weekly Super Smash Bros. Project M tournaments, featuring Harmak, who's pretty good at this video game.
When: Every Friday
8:00 PM
Where: UCrossing 158 - Get Directions
Parking: As you look at the front entrance of UCrossing, there is a parking lot to the right, across Salem Street, a small side street. After 5:00 PM, this lot is open to the public. If the gate is down, press the call button at the intercom and ask them to let you in.
Food: Crossroads Cafe is right in the same building, featuring Sal's Pizza. Options include pizza, sandwiches/wraps, salads, and pastries. You can probably expect to spend ~$10 here. Right next to the cafe is a Starbucks, where you can get gelato, and a mini convenience store, where you can get other snacks/drinks.

*WE WILL HAVE SIGNS FOR YOU TO FOLLOW*
The easiest way to find UCrossing room 158 is to enter at the back of the building, at the UCAPS/University Police entrance. This is across the street from the parking lot and UCrossing bus stop. From this entrance, follow the hallway (near the stairs) to the right, and find a door labeled 150. The tournament room is just to the right from here.
If you enter from the front entrance of UCrossing, your path will be a little more complicated. You will need to go up the main stairway in the middle, continue straight and cross the bridge to your left to get to the B Wing. Take the stairs on your right down two floors, and follow the hallway straight ahead, and find a door labeled 150. The tournament room is just to the right from here.

SkillKeeper

If you're interested in seeing more detailed statistics, take a look at the SkillKeeper stats. If you've entered any UML Weeklies at all since Spring 2017, you can see your matchup history with every other player you have played in one convenient location.

Download

After downloading, simply unzip the folder, run SkillKeeper.exe, select "Load World" and choose the semester you would like to view from in the Project M folder. "current.bcn" contains all semesters since we began tracking it. From there you can find your personal stats by selecting your tag under the "View/Modify Player" tab.
