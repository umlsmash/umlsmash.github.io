SkillKeeper

If you're interested in seeing more detailed statistics, take a look at the Fall 2016 SkillKeeper world. If you've entered any UML Weeklies at all for that semester, you can see your matchup history with every other player you have played in one convenient location.

Download

After downloading, simply unzip the folder, run SkillKeeper.exe, select "Load World" and choose UMLMeleeSkillKeeperFall2016.bcn. From there you can find your personal stats by selecting your tag under the "View/Modify Player" tab.
