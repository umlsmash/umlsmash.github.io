Details

EPIC is hosting a multi-game charity tournament in collaboration with UMLSmash on Saturday, March 25th. The tournament will featured Super Smash Bros. Melee Singles and Doubles, Super Smash Bros. for Wii U Singles and Doubles, and Wii Boxing.
This tournament will take place in USuites 106. A link to the location is provided below if you need help finding it.
Check out the Facebook event page for more info!

If you'd like to compete in this event, please click the button below and fill out the form. You can either pay your entry fee at our table in University Crossing 3/20 - 3/24 or you can pay it at the door as you enter the tournament venue.
