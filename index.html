Welcome to UML Smash!

This website organizes information for UMass Lowell's Super Smash Bros. club, including important links (brackets, live streams, etc) and power rankings for each game.

Games

SSB Melee

What: Our free-to-enter (no payout) weekly Super Smash Bros. Melee tournaments, usually featuring either draft crew battles or a doubles bracket before (and in some cases, after) singles.
When: Every Friday
~6:00 PM (doubles/crews)
~7:15 (singles)
Where: USuites - Get Directions
Tournament Officials
Head:
Nathan Cordeiro
(BlueEco)
Other:
Sam Chambers
(sambuddy5)
Other:
Scott Mcafee
(NoTechSkill)
Parking: There are metered spots on the side street to the right of USuites (as you are looking at it from the front). These meters aren't generally checked after 6:00 PM. If you get there any earlier, you should be fine if you pay $1 to the meter.
Food: You can get eat at Hawk's Nest (right down the hall from the venue room!). They have chicken fingers and the like, as well as chicken friend rice and lo mein. If you're planning on eating here, plan on spending ~$10 or less. If you're in the mood, they also have smoothies and frozen yogurt!
Go to SSBM page

SSB 4

What: Our weekly Super Smash Bros. 4 tournaments, which includes free singles and doubles, twitch stream, and a personally designed trophy for the winner.
When: Every Monday
~8:00 PM
Where: UCrossing - Get Directions
Tournament Officials
Head:
Seth Kary
(BigSweetPeePee)
Other:
Matt Hayes
(HaZe)
Other:
Brandon Le
(Fat)
Parking: As you look at the front entrance of UCrossing, there is a parking lot to the right, across Salem Street, a small side street. After 5:00 PM, this lot is open to the public. If the gate is down, press the call button at the intercom and ask them to let you in.
Food: Crossroads Cafe is right downstairs, featuring Sal's Pizza. Options include pizza, sandwiches/wraps, salads, and pastries. You can probably expect to spend ~$10 here. Right next to the cafe is a Starbucks, where you can get gelato, and a mini convenience store, where you can get other snacks/drinks.
