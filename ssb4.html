Super Smash Bros. 4

Info

What: Our weekly Super Smash Bros. 4 tournaments, which includes free singles and doubles, twitch stream, and a personally designed trophy for the winner.
When: Every Monday
~8:00 PM
Where: UCrossing - Get Directions
Tournament Officials
Head:
Seth Kary
(BigSweetPeePee)
Other:
Matt Hayes
(HaZe)
Other:
Brandon Le
(Fat)
Parking: As you look at the front entrance of UCrossing, there is a parking lot to the right, across Salem Street, a small side street. After 5:00 PM, this lot is open to the public. If the gate is down, press the call button at the intercom and ask them to let you in.
Food: Crossroads Cafe is right downstairs, featuring Sal's Pizza. Options include pizza, sandwiches/wraps, salads, and pastries. You can probably expect to spend ~$10 here. Right next to the cafe is a Starbucks, where you can get gelato, and a mini convenience store, where you can get other snacks/drinks.

SkillKeeper

If you're interested in seeing more detailed statistics, take a look at the SkillKeeper stats. If you've entered any UML Weeklies at all since Spring 2017, you can see your matchup history with every other player you have played in one convenient location.

Download

After downloading, simply unzip the folder, run SkillKeeper.exe, select "Load World" and choose the semester you would like to view from in the Smash 4 folder. "current.bcn" contains all semesters since we began tracking it. From there you can find your personal stats by selecting your tag under the "View/Modify Player" tab.
